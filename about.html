about

what is acmCSUF?

acmCSUF is a student-ran chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery.
You may call us acm for short! We focus on introducing and getting students involved in tech,
as well as educating students about the field through workshops, presentations, livestreams,
and more. Our events are open to any and everyone, so be sure to come by!
